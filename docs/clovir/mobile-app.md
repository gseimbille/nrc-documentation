You can view the dashboard on your phone using the Datacake app:
- iPhone: `https://apps.apple.com/us/app/datacake/id1358497267`
- Android: `https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.datacakehq.datacakedashboardapp`

In the app:
1. Remove demo devices in **Settings** > **Remove devices**.
2. Add a new device by tapping the **+** icon on the home screen.
3. Scan the QR code generated when you shared the dashboard.
